Vacancy and Personnel custom fields

With this feature you can define a boilerplate for the Unpublished Job Details field in the PRS Vacancies form and the Synopsis field on the Personnel Summary form.

 

Here is an example scenario: On all our contract vacancies we need to record the name of the supervisor to report to (not necessarily the client) plus a client order number. First we need to create a boilerplate for the Unpublished Job Details Vacancies field (which is where we will record the additional vacancy details).
