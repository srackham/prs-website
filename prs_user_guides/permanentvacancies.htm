Permanent Vacancies

A Permanent vacancy is characterised by a one-off client fee usually for placing a candidate in a permanent position.

 

 

Vacancy Item

Description

Salary

The candidate salary. This is entered when the vacancy is registered and is not necessarily the final placed salary. Often the low end of the salary range for vacancy publication purposes when used in conjunction with the Up to field.

Fee

The placement fee agreed to by the client. This entry does not have to be entered or finalised till the placement is made.

Up to

The maximum possible salary that this position can command.

 

Salary and Fee items are not used in financial billings reports, billing information comes from related client invoices.
